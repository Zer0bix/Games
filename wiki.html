Clicking

Tree clicking
Formula:
floor((sqrt(clicks_per_sec)) * (1 + tree_levels + click_building_effect))
Explanation:
Takes the square root of how many clicks you have, and multiplies it by the sum of 1, your tree level and the power of the click building. It then rounds this down to the nearest whole number.
This is added on top of the already existing tree production.

Stone Clicking
Formula:
(0.1 * clicks_per_sec * tree_levels) * click_stone_building_effect
Explanation:
Multiplies the clicks per second by your tree level, then multiplies that by 0.1, all multiplied by the power of the stone click building.
This is added on top of the already existing stone production.

Buildings

Formulae
Price: cost = (1.01^(buildings_owned_total) * 1/(1+0.1*reduce_costs.owned)) * 1.5 ^ this_building_owned
Explanation:
For every building of any type owned, multiply the cost of this buildings by 1.01. Divide this by 1 plus a tenth of the owned reduce_costs building, so 10 owned means half, 20 owned means a third etc. For every building of this type owned, multiply the cost of this building by 1.5

Effect: effect = 1 + owned * multi
Explanation:
Multiply how many of a building you own by the building's multi. Add one to this to prevent values of less than 1 from occuring, which would cause either 0 production, or cause a division by 0 (resulting in a math error)

Justification of Theme
I chose to make a website based on the concept of Idle/Incremental games. The website would have a main webpage hosting the game, a cps tester (with square root of cps) to see how the click speed affected production, and a wiki to inform the user on the hidden mechanics and fomulae of the game. The website is designed to be able to be used by anyone of any experience, and is designed to be enjoyable. People will come to my website primarily for the game, with the cps test and wiki in addition to the game.
